Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to deliver and improve our services — such as your name, phone number, email address, and service preferences. We never request unnecessary personal data.

2. How We Use Information

Information you provide is used to schedule visits, personalize support, and improve your experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Protection

Your information is stored securely using encrypted systems. Access is limited to authorized Click & Care personnel for legitimate service purposes only.

4. Communication

We may contact you regarding appointment confirmations, service updates, or feedback requests. You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

5. Cancellations & Data Handling

Appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance with no fee. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a small service fee to cover technician time. Any information shared before cancellation remains protected under this policy.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality and performance tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be announced on our website.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us at clickncareservice@gmail.com.
